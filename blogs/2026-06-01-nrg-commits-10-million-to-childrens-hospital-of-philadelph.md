NRG Energy has committed $10 million to Children's Hospital of Philadelphia's Cardiac Center through its Choose to Give program, establishing both an endowed fund for long-term support and an innovation fund for immediate cardiac research and clinical initiatives. Since 2016, the program has funded over 30 projects supporting treatments for conditions like pulmonary hypertension and advanced cardiac care for children with heart disease.
